Our Mission

At Varian, a Siemens Healthineers company, our goal is to envision a world without fear of cancer. Through innovative cancer care technologies, we empower clinical partners globally to treat millions of patients annually.

Optimize Radiation Therapy Treatments

Utilizing advanced AI and machine learning, our system optimizes the scheduling of cancer treatments across multiple linear accelerators, ensuring efficient and equitable use of resources.

Linear Accelerators at Your Fingertips

Choose the most suitable linear accelerator for each patient based on treatment requirements:

  • VitalBeam 1 - Versatile and robust for diverse treatment needs.
  • VitalBeam 2 - Ideal for complex cases, offering precision and flexibility.
  • TrueBeam 1 & TrueBeam 2 - Advanced technology for high-precision treatments.
  • Unique Clinac - Streamlined and efficient for specific treatment types.
